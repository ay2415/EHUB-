The Digilent PCAM (short for "Peripheral Camera") is a popular camera module designed and manufactured by Digilent, a company known for its development boards and accessories for embedded systems and FPGA (Field-Programmable Gate Array) development. The Digilent PCAM is primarily intended for use with Digilent's FPGA development boards, such as the Zybo and Zedboard, to enable developers to integrate camera functionality into their FPGA-based projects.
